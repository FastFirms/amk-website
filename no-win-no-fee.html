01

What Does No Win No Fee Actually Mean?

It means you pay nothing to start your claim, nothing while it runs, and no legal fees at all if it doesn't succeed. Fees and outlays are only paid from a successful settlement — so the financial risk sits with us, not you.

We also pay the out-of-pocket expenses — medical reports, expert opinions, court fees — as the claim progresses. At the end, you're invoiced only for the actual expense incurred: no margins added, no surprises.

02

Do You Pay Anything If You Lose?

At AMK: no legal fees, full stop. Be careful elsewhere — some 'No Win, No Fee' agreements still let the firm recover outlays from you if the claim fails, or load the file with litigation lending and interest.

Read any cost agreement before you sign it, ask what happens to outlays on a loss, and ask whether interest accrues. If the answers are vague, that's your answer. We'll review another firm's agreement for you, free, before you commit.

03

What Is The 50/50 Rule In Queensland?

A legal cap on what a law firm can charge in speculative personal injury matters: after refunds and outlays, your lawyer's fees cannot exceed half of what's left. You can never walk away with less than half your net compensation.

It's a floor, not a target — our fees come in under the cap in the great majority of claims. But the rule matters because it's your statutory protection regardless of who you engage, and any firm reluctant to explain it is telling you something.

04

How Are AMK's Fees Lower Than The Big Firms?

We're a boutique team focused only on compensation law — no franchise overheads, no TV advertising budgets baked into your bill, no juniors learning on your file. Streamlined claims cost less to run, and that difference lands in your pocket.

Lower fees with senior attention isn't a contradiction; it's the point of staying small. See why clients choose us for the full picture.

05

What Are Uplift Fees — And Why Don't We Charge Them?

An uplift fee is a success premium — up to 25% added on top of normal fees — that many firms charge for taking the 'risk' of No Win, No Fee. We don't charge uplift fees, and we don't charge interest on outlays either.

The risk argument rings hollow when claims are properly assessed before they're taken on. We'd rather win you more and keep the arrangement clean: one transparent Cost Agreement, in plain English, before anything starts.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes — completely. We'll assess your claim, explain your rights and time limits, and give you a realistic view of prospects and value. No obligation, no pressure to proceed, no invoice.

What claims are covered by No Win No Fee?

Every compensation claim we run: car and CTP claims, WorkCover and common law work injury claims, public liability, medical negligence, institutional abuse and TPD/superannuation claims.

Who pays the other side's costs if I lose in court?

Adverse costs are only a live risk if a claim goes to court and fails — which is rare, because the pre-court process resolves most claims and we don't run claims to trial that shouldn't go there. We explain this risk frankly, in writing, before any proceedings start.

Can I switch to AMK from another No Win No Fee firm?

Yes — clients change lawyers more often than you'd think, usually over communication or fee concerns. We'll review your existing agreement for free and explain exactly how a transfer works before you decide.

How do I know what I'll actually receive in the end?

Before you accept any settlement, we give you a written breakdown: gross settlement, refunds, outlays, fees, and the net amount in your hand. You approve the settlement knowing the real number — never after the fact.
