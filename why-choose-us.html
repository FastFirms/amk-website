About AMK

We Started On The Other Side Of The Fence.

Our founder and principal, Michael, started AMK Lawyers in 2013 after years representing insurers defending personal injury claims — including running court trials. He gained valuable insight into exactly how insurers defend these claims, and discovered that helping injured people win compensation was far more gratifying than helping insurers avoid paying it.

AMK is an abbreviation of Michael's family members' names — and he's applied the same attitude to how he views the firm and its clients. He's built a friendly, down-to-earth and welcoming team that is fearless in fighting for the just and fair compensation you deserve.

More than ten years on, AMK Lawyers services all of Queensland with advice customised to your personal circumstances. We're beside you through every stage of your claim, we explain every part of the process so there are no surprises, and if you can't visit one of our offices, we'll come to you — at home, by phone or by video. We're here to get to know you, support you, and make sure you understand your rights.

Our Commitment To You

Six Reasons People Choose AMK.

01

100% No Win, No Fee — With Lower Fees

$0 upfrontNo uplift feesNo interest on outlays

Every claim we take on is covered by our No Win, No Fee guarantee, no matter your financial situation. Other firms can still recover their outlays if your claim goes pear-shaped — at AMK, unless you receive compensation, we do not charge you a cent.

There are no expensive litigation lending arrangements, no interest on outlays, and no uplift fees. We pay the out-of-pocket expenses along the way, and at the end of your claim you're only invoiced for the actual expense — no hidden fees or charges. And because of our experience and streamlined process, our fee structure is much lower than the large franchise firms. We don't take expensive detours to get you a result.

02

100+ Years' Combined Experience

Both sides of the fence24/7 accessNo juniors or paralegals

You'll be personally represented by someone with a wealth of knowledge in Queensland compensation law — not handed to a junior or paralegal like at the franchise firms.

Our staff have experience representing insurers, so we know both sides of the fence — the approach and tactics insurers use when defending claims. We stay across case law and industry developments so we're always ahead of the game, we maintain regular contact every step of the way, and you'll have 24/7 access to the knowledge of the whole firm. Because we care for your wellbeing as much as the compensation you deserve.

03

Focused On Compensation Law — Keeping It Simple

One focusNo jargonExpert network

Pursuing compensation claims is all we do. By focusing only on this area of law, we're never distracted from the job at hand — maximising your compensation.

The law can be complicated, so we simplify it. You'll understand the process at every step, without the legal jargon. And our strong network of experts and professionals in the personal injury field means you always have the best advice when making informed decisions about your claim.

04

Free Case Appraisal

No obligationWe come to youPaperless

Uncertain if you have a claim? Confused about the process? One of our experienced lawyers will discuss your potential claim at no obligation and give you a road map of the process moving forward.

We'll take the time to hear your story, and our no-nonsense approach means honest, upfront advice. Sit down with us, or we'll arrange a home visit, video conference or phone call — we're a paperless office and can represent you no matter where you are in Queensland.

05

Compassionate And Caring, With A Rehabilitation Focus

Rehab firstAt no cost to you

Being injured and struggling to make ends meet is filled with uncertainty. Our lawyers are compassionate and caring, providing a personal touch and direction through troubled waters.

It's paramount to us that you access rehabilitation at the earliest opportunity — at no cost to you. Treatment like physiotherapy, psychology, radiology, specialists and occupational rehabilitation is critical to identify, diagnose and treat your injuries for the best possible recovery. We're committed to getting you back to your normal self.
